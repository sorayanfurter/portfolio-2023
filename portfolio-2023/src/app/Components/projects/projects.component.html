PORTAL DE EXAMENES

Built using Angular/Java and designed for creating and taking multiple-choice exams. Admins, who are typically professors, will be have able to create exams, add questions with their respective scores, and set time limits for students to answer. Additionally, admins can organize the exams into different categories for easy management. On the other hand, users, who are students, can log in to the app and take the exams available to them.
